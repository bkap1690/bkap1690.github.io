A Little Bit About Myself

Brett is a Certified UX Designer who creates user-centered experiences through innovative design.

​

Brett believes in using a balanced approach of research, design principles, and empathy to create the best possible experience for his client and their target user.

​

After working in Marketing where he strategized and executed digital campaigns, Brett transitioned into UX Design. This shift allowed him to merge his experience in Marketing with his passion for design and content creation.

​

After encountering many frustrating user experiences while working as a Marketer, Brett set out into the UX world with the goal of creating more enjoyable, accessible experiences for users.

​

As a Production Specialist at Activision, he has helped redesign the mobile navigation for the top performing site, created and executed testing strategy, and redesigned the Games page on Activision's website.

​

Recently, he has also worked with RELAYTO, where he redesigned their platform UI that renders PDF documents into presentations.

​

Brett grew up in New York, but currently resides in Los Angeles. He graduated from the University of Rhode Island in 2014 and earned his UX certification through the online learning platform, Springboard. In his spare time, Brett loves traveling, playing basketball, and honing his design skills.
