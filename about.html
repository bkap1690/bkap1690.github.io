A Little Bit About Myself

As a UX Designer, I create user-centered experiences through innovative design.

​

I believe in using a balanced approach of research, design principles, and empathy to create the best possible experience for my client and the target user.

​

After working in Marketing, where I strategized and executed digital campaigns, I transitioned into UX Design. This shift allowed me to merge my experience in Marketing with my passion for design and content creation.

​

After encountering many frustrating user experiences while working as a Marketer, I set out into the UX world to create more enjoyable, accessible experiences for users.

​

As an Optimization Specialist at Activision, I lead testing initiatives across all brand websites to implement designs proven to drive traffic and conversions.

​

I have recently also worked with RELAYTO, where I redesigned their platform UI that renders PDF documents into online presentations.

​

I grew up in New York but currently reside in Los Angeles. I graduated from the University of Rhode Island and earned a UX certification through Springboard's online learning platform. In my spare time, I love traveling, playing basketball, and honing my design skills.
